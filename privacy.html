Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Idle Space Mining collects no personal data.

What is collected

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that gather information. It does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photos, because it does not use them.

Where your game data lives

Your progress is saved in a file on your own device. It is never uploaded anywhere. It is included in your device backup if you back up your device, and deleting the app deletes it.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. Apple tells the app whether a purchase succeeded — nothing more. Payment details never reach this app or its developer. Apple's handling of that data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Network access

The app makes no network requests of its own. The only network traffic is Apple's own StoreKit framework talking to the App Store when you open the shop or make a purchase.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this ever changes — for example if analytics or leaderboards are added — this policy will be updated before the feature ships, and the App Store privacy labels will be updated to match.
