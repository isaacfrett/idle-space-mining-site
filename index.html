Idle Space Mining

Automate, ascend, repeat.

Tap an asteroid. Build a mining empire. Then collapse it into a singularity and come back stronger.

What it is

An idle game about scale. You start hand-mining ore from a bare rock and end up rewriting local physics so the ore was always there. Twelve generators, each running its own harvest cycle. Every ten you own doubles that generator's output.

Doctrines

At each tier you pick one of three paths and forfeit the other two — and you re-decide every run. There is no correct answer.

Ascension

When growth stalls, collapse the colony. You lose your ore, your generators and your tech. You keep Dark Matter, and every scrap you have ever earned makes you permanently stronger. Then there is a prestige above the prestige.

No dark patterns

No ads. No energy timers. No countdown “offers”. The game is never unplayable until you pay.
